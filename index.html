Built for People Who Know People

Solar rooftop adoption in India is accelerating — but most homeowners, factory owners, and businesses never act on it because no one in their circle brings it up at the right moment. That's where you come in. Solarpreneur is an influencer-led referral program for solar rooftop projects. You don't install panels. You don't pitch technical specs. You simply connect people in your network who could benefit from solar — to us. We handle everything: site survey, system design, financing, installation, and DISCOM approvals. You earn ₹1000 per kW on every deal that closes from your referral.
